About Bra Nox Primary School Transport

The village trusted school transport. Bra Nox has over 10 years of experience in school transport services.

Norman Letlape, affectionately known as Bra Nox, is a respected transport operator in his village and surrounding areas.

With over 35 years of experience in the taxi association industry, he has built a reputation not only as a reliable driver but also as a dedicated member of his community.Bra Nox began his career as a taxi driver, transporting people to and from town.

His passion for public transport and commitment to serving people led him to identify a critical issue in his community: the lack of safe, child-friendly school transport services. Parents were concerned about their children’s safety, reliability, and punctuality when using available transport options.

Motivated by compassion and a strong sense of responsibility, Bra Nox launched a school transport service that families could depend on.

What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted lifeline for many households, providing consistent, safe transport for learners across the area.Known for his punctuality, discipline, and love for children, Bra Nox treats every child like family.

He ensures they are picked up and dropped off on time, always with care and respect.

His calm demeanor and friendly attitude have made him a favorite among both learners and their parents.

To Bra Nox, transporting children isn’t just a job, it’s a calling. He believes that a strong community begins with supporting its youngest members, and he lives that belief every single day because he believes that he Drives bright upcoming future generation "#Driven to deliver bright futures".

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, affordable and punctual school transport for children in the community and also to give parents peace of mind and knowing their children are in trustworthy hands

Our Vision

To become the most trusted local school transport provider by delivering exceptional service with care, integrity, and consistency.

Fun Fact about Bra Nox Annual September Free Trip!

Bra Nox School Transport takes children on a free trip every year during September holidays!. This is more than just a fun outing; it’s an important part of Bra Nox’s commitment to the children he serves.

By taking learners to places like Rustenburg Show Grounds or Pilanesberg Nature Reserve, Bra Nox provides them with opportunities they might not otherwise have. These trips are vital for a child’s development as they:

Create lasting memories: Giving them a chance to simply be kids, creating happy memories that they will cherish for years to come.

Encourage social bonding: Allowing children to build stronger friendships and connections with their peers in a relaxed setting.
