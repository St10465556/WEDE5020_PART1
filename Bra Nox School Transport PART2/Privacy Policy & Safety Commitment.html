Privacy Policy & Safety Commitment

Privacy Policy

At Bra Nox primary school transport, we value your privacy. We only collect necessary and reliable information to provide safe and efficient school transportation for your child.

Information We Collect:

  • Child's full name, age and school name
  • Parent or guardian contact details
  • Pick-up and drop-off locations
  • Emergency contact information
Click here to enter Parent/Guardian Contact Details




How we use information

  • To arrange safe and timely transportation
  • To communicate important updates or emergencies
  • To process payments and maintain records

Data Protection[POPI Act]:

Your child's information is stored securely and only accessible to authorized staff. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties.

Safety Commitment

We are committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of every child who uses our transport service.#Driven to deliver bright futures.

Our Safety Practices:

  • Background-checked and trained drivers
  • Regular vehicle maintenance and safety inspections
  • Seat belts and child-friendly transport protocols
  • GPS tracking on all transport vehicles
  • Emergency response plans and contact systems
